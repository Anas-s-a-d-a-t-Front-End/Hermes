Located in the Nile City Towers by the River Nile, this 5-star hotel features a rooftop pool deck with stunning views across Cairo to the Pyramids. It offers 7 places to eat and drink. All rooms at Fairmont Nile City are soundproofed and decorated with Art Deco furnishings. They are equipped with a work desk and interactive IPTV. Bathrooms have a rain shower with sliding shutters. Some rooms offer panoramic views of the city or the Nile. Fairmont Nile City offers modern equipment in the spacious fitness centre. A well-equipped business center is also available on site. Fairmont Nile City is only 23.5 km from Cairo International Airport and Cairo Museum is a 10-minute drive away. The Nile Towers Mall is also steps away from the hotel.
